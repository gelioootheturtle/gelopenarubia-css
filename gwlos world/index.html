Why I Chose CSS

I chose Computer Systems Servicing because technology has always been the one thing that keeps my curiosity active. I grew up noticing how devices can fail for simple reasons and how one correct fix can bring a system back to life. I was also influenced by my brother who works in the IT field, and seeing that environment made me believe that I could follow a similar path while still building my own identity. Even if I sometimes feel pressure to be practical, I did not choose CSS just to “have something.” I chose it because I want to be capable and reliable in real situations where people need answers, not excuses.

Deep down, my dream is still science. Astronomy, biology, and botany are the fields that truly pull my attention because they make me think about life, time, and the universe in a deeper way. But opportunities are not always equal, and I learned early that being practical matters. So I decided to take a path that is realistic now, while keeping the part of me that dreams. CSS is not the end of my story. It is the foundation that helps me survive, learn, and stay ready for bigger goals.
