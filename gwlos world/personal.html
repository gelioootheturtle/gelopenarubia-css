About Me

My name is Angelo F. Peñarubia. I became interested in technology because I have always been curious about how things work behind the screen. I was the type of person who could not ignore a problem. If a computer was slow, if a program was crashing, or if a system was not booting properly, I wanted to understand the reason instead of accepting it as normal. Over time, that curiosity became a habit. I started paying attention to the details that most people skip, and that mindset helped me learn technical work faster.

I was also influenced by my brother who is in the IT field. Watching someone close to me deal with real systems and real issues made me realize that this path is not just about gadgets. It is about responsibility. It is about being the person who stays calm when something fails and who can bring order back when everything becomes messy. I chose Computer Systems Servicing because it is practical and because it gives me a strong base. Even if my path looks similar to my brother’s, I am not copying his life. I am using inspiration and building my own version of it.

At the same time, I am honest about my dreams. I want to be a scientist, especially in astronomy, biology, and botany. Those fields feel personal to me because they remind me that the world is bigger than stress, deadlines, and limitations. But opportunity is not always easy, and I learned that being practical matters. So I chose IT as my present path because it gives stability and clear skills that I can use now, while I keep the part of me that still wants to explore science. I do not see this as giving up. I see it as choosing survival while still protecting my future goals.
