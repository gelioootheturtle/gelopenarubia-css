Sir Lawrence B. Esquejo

Sir Lawrence B. Esquejo is a TESDA trainer and a highly skilled teacher who treats technical training as something serious and real. His teaching is structured, practical, and focused on correct procedures instead of shortcuts. During hands-on sessions, he is calm and composed, and that makes students feel guided rather than pressured. He explains tasks with clarity, and when a student struggles, he does not embarrass them. He helps them understand what went wrong and how to correct it.

What I respect most is how he teaches with confidence while still being approachable. His style makes CSS feel like a real professional environment. He encourages students to develop discipline, accuracy, and responsibility, which are the most important habits in technical work. Because of his training approach, I learned to value proper troubleshooting steps, documentation, and careful handling of systems instead of rushing just to finish.
